STAY SAFE ONLINE – CHECKLIST


✅ General Safety Tips
✔ Never share OTP or passwords with anyone

✔ Do not click on unknown links or messages

✔ Verify website URLs before entering details

✔ Use strong and unique passwords

✔ Enable two-factor authentication

💰 UPI / PAYMENT SAFETY (IMPORTANT)
✔ Never enter UPI PIN to receive money

✔ Always check whether it is a “Pay” or “Request” notification

📱 Awareness & Reporting
✔ Keep apps and devices updated

✔ Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions

✔ Immediately report fraud to National Cyber Crime Portal

✔ Call Helpline 1930 for financial fraud
